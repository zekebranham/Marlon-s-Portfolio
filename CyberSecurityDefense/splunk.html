Splunk Cyber Defense Analyst Skills

Overview

My journey to achieving the Splunk Certified Cyber Defense Analyst Certification has been a transformative experience. Having completed 10 out of the 15 required courses, I have gained critical insights into the processes and thought patterns of a Cyber Defense Analyst, particularly in threat detection, analysis, and response.

I am actively learning and practicing a broad array of frameworks, attack surfaces, and defense methodologies. Below is a detailed outline of my current knowledge and progress in these areas.

Key Frameworks and Standards

  • CVE Program: Prioritizing mitigation efforts based on known vulnerabilities.
  • MITRE ATT&CK Enterprise Matrix: Studying adversary tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs).
  • Pyramid of Pain: Understanding how deeper indicators can improve threat detection.
  • Lockheed Martin Cyber Kill Chain: Learning the phases of an attack to improve proactive defense.
  • Regulatory Standards: Exploring GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS for compliance training.
  • ISO 27000 & NIST Frameworks: Learning how to apply governance and risk controls.
  • OWASP Top Ten: Identifying and mitigating top web vulnerabilities.

Understanding and Analyzing Attack Surfaces

  • Phishing & Typesquatting: Analyzing deceptive strategies used in credential harvesting.
  • DNS Attacks: Recognizing and mitigating tunneling, amplification, and spoofing.
  • Malware Analysis: Exploring behaviors of ransomware, adware, and trojans.
  • Supply Chain Risks: Understanding third-party threats and dependency vulnerabilities.
  • Indicators of Compromise: Tracking key forensic artifacts of compromise.

Core Defense Analyst Skills

  • TTP Identification: Applying MITRE ATT&CK knowledge in real-time analysis.
  • SIEM Usage: Practicing Splunk Enterprise Security queries and dashboard building.
  • SOC Understanding: Studying Tier 1/2/3 analyst roles and escalation procedures.

Reflection

While I am still on the journey, the Splunk coursework has greatly expanded my knowledge of the modern cyber defense landscape. Through exercises, case studies, and labs, I have grown in confidence as an analyst and look forward to completing certification.
